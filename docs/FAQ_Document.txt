ABC Technologies - FAQ Document

Q: How do I reset my password?
A: Go to the login page and click "Forgot Password". A reset link will be sent to your registered email.

Q: How can I update my profile?
A: Log in, go to Settings > Profile, and make your changes there.

Q: How do I activate or deactivate my account?
A: Go to Settings > Account Management. You can deactivate your account there. Account closure/deletion requires reaching out to support for human approval.

Q: Where can I find my invoice?
A: Invoices are available under Settings > Billing > Invoice History.

Q: How do I change my payment method?
A: Go to Settings > Billing > Payment Methods to add or update your credit card details.
