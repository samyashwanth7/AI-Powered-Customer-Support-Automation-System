ABC Technologies - Company Policy Document

1. Returns and Refunds
- Refund requests for annual subscriptions must be reviewed and approved by management.
- Refunds are generally only provided within the first 30 days of purchase.
- Monthly subscriptions are non-refundable once the billing cycle starts.

2. Account Closure
- Account closure requests require a manager's approval.
- We retain customer data for 30 days post-closure unless a hard delete is requested.

3. Escalations
- Any compensation requests or complaints about service must be escalated to a supervisor.
- Critical technical issues affecting all users are immediately escalated to Tier 3 support.
