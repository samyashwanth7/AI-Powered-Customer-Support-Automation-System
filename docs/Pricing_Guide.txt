ABC Technologies - Pricing Guide

1. Basic Plan
- $19/month per user
- Includes core SaaS features, 5GB storage, email support.

2. Professional Plan
- $49/month per user
- Includes all Basic features, 50GB storage, Priority email & chat support, advanced analytics.

3. Enterprise Plan
- Custom pricing (Starts at $99/month per user)
- Unlimited storage, 24/7 dedicated support, custom integrations.

Note: Annual subscriptions receive a 20% discount compared to month-to-month billing.
